About Us

Dorylus Travel Adventures (DTA) is a conservation-inspired travel agency that offers curated experiences that showcase the best of Rwanda and the East Africa region. Our aim is to provide inspiring travel experiences that leave a positive impact on both the traveler and the destinations we explore.

Our Story

The life - changing presentation in October 2023 about ants made the founder realize the importance of protecting these fascinating creatures. Conservation efforts for ants should begin b y gaining a comprehensive understanding of all species through research. Based on empirical evidence, conservation initiatives can then be implemented. When the founder inquired about the presenter's future plans, they turned the question around and asked about his . Given the limited scientific background for initiating research projects, the founder reflected on his own experiences and came up with the idea of opening a travel agency. Drawing from over 10 years of experience in the travel industry, they aim ed to combine the passion for conservation practices with the creation of this agency. The agency's name, Dorylus Travel Adventures, was inspired by the popular driver ants, also known as siafu or safari ants. DTA's goal is to organize trips to Rwanda and the surrounding region.Part of the net revenue generated from these travels will be dedicated to supporting ant research..

Our being & our doing

We believe that travel can be a genuine force for good. That’s why we’ re committed to enrich lives through inspiring travel experiences that leave a positive impact on both the traveler and the destinations we explore. We are committed to supporting the study of the ants through building local knowledge and understanding of ant biodiversity in Rwanda and the region. It is believed that Rwanda harbors more than 600 species with only 147 species taxonomy discovered so far. The taxonomy of the other 453 species is unknown. We are dedicated to creating unique and immersive journeys that contribute to the scientifi c research of the ant biodiversity in Rwanda and the region. Through our expertly curated itineraries, we aim to ignite a passion for understanding and conserving the vital role ants play in our ecosystem. With a commitment to sustainability and responsibl e travel, we strive to showcase the wonders of nature fostering a sense of awe and respect for wildlife and more particularly the ant kingdom; tiny yet remarkable creatures.

Rwanda as a Tourist Destination

Renowned as the "land of a thousand hills," Rwanda beckons with its captivating beauty and hospitable locals, providing travelers with unparalleled experiences in one of the world's most extraordinary destinations. This enchanting country is a treasure trove of biodiversity, boasting a rich tapestry of wildlife thriving amidst its volcanic landscapes, lush montane rainforests, and vast plains, promising unforgettable encounters with nature's wonders at every turn.

The government has placed conservation and environmental protections as a central policy alongside socioeconomic development, with a healthy environment recognized as a fundamental human right. Nature-based tourism of the four national parks was strategically identified as a key economic contributor in income and employment.

The tourism circuit consists of:

  • Kigali city tour including hiking trails
  • Rwanda National Parks (four)
  • Kivu belt
  • Museums and Historical places
Nyungwe National Park

Nyungwe has incredibly high wildlife diversity and endemism, making it a priority for conservation. In September 2023, this forest was inscribed as UNESCO Heritage Site, acknowledging its universal value and the need to protect it for the benefit of all humanity.
